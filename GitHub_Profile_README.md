## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Senior Software Architect** with **7+ years of experience** designing, scaling, and leading cloud-native SaaS platforms, API-driven systems, e-commerce solutions, and enterprise applications.

I work where **architecture, engineering leadership, and business outcomes** meet—turning complex requirements into secure, scalable, and maintainable platforms.

- 🏗️ Designing SaaS, multi-tenant, API-first, and event-driven architectures
- ☁️ Leading cloud infrastructure and production operations across GCP and AWS
- 🔐 Applying DevSecOps, security reviews, observability, and infrastructure hardening
- 📊 Building analytics pipelines with Pub/Sub, BigQuery, and business dashboards
- 👥 Mentoring engineers and improving architecture, delivery, and code-review standards
- 🌱 Growing deeper into Cloud Architecture, Platform Engineering, and DevSecOps Architecture
